Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services.

How long does approval take?

Approval timelines vary depending on the complexity of your case and the specific facility required. However, due to our streamlined process and strong banking network, we typically secure preliminary approvals within 3 to 7 working days.

Is consultation free?

Yes, your initial consultation is completely free and carries no obligation. We use this time to assess your situation and determine if we can add value to your financial journey.

What documents are required?

Generally, we require standard identification, income proofs (such as salary slips or audited accounts for businesses), bank statements, and credit reports. A specific checklist will be provided after your initial assessment.

Can you assist businesses with cash flow challenges?

Absolutely. We specialize in SME growth and restructuring. We can identify working capital solutions, invoice financing, or debt restructuring options to stabilize and improve your cash flow.

Is my information confidential?

We adhere to strict data privacy policies (PDPA compliant). All your personal and financial information is treated with the highest level of security and confidentiality.
