What Banks Look For Before Approving Your Financing Application

Applying for financing in Malaysia can feel like a stressful waiting game. You submit a stack of documents and wait days, or even weeks, for the credit committee's decision. But bank approvals are not a mystery—credit officers evaluate profiles using a highly structured set of parameters.

If you want to secure high approval rates and favorable terms, understanding what banks look for is the key to presenting an elite credit profile.

1. Debt Service Ratio (DSR)

DSR is the single most critical mathematical formula banks use to evaluate affordability. It calculates what percentage of your net monthly income goes toward settling bank commitments:

DSR = (Total Monthly Bank Commitments / Net Monthly Income) x 100%

In Malaysia, most retail banks prefer a DSR below 60% to 70%. If your DSR is too high, the credit system will flag you as overcommitted, leading to automatic rejection regardless of a good payment history.

2. CCRIS & CTOS Report Track Record

Credit reports act as your financial report card. Banks examine CCRIS for active arrears, patterns of late payments, and the count of recent loan applications. They also pull CTOS reports to verify if you have pending legal suits, bankruptcy declarations, or trade references (such as outstanding utility bills).

3. Employment & Income Stability

Banks look for steady cash inflows. For salaried employees, this means at least 3 to 6 months of continuous employment with EPF contributions. For self-employed individuals, banks require a registered business operating for at least 2 years, supported by tax declarations (Form B/BE) and audited financial accounts.

Did you know? Casual income, cash sales without bank statements, or undocumented allowances are usually discounted or ignored by credit assessors when calculating DSR.

4. Documentation Packages

Incomplete documentation is a major cause of delays. Ensure your payslips match your bank statements exactly down to the cent, and that your bank statements are clearly formatted without missing pages.

Summary

Presenting a clean DSR, clear bank statements, and a healthy credit score will put your application in the fast lane. If you are unsure whether your profile meets these parameters, let the advisory team at NEX Advisory Solutions review your situation first to enhance your application package.
